About Me

The person behind the work

I didn't start in tech. I started in fast-paced agency work in India, managing digital campaigns for some of the biggest brands and Bollywood films in the country. Tight timelines, high stakes, and a lot of moving parts — that's where I learned how to keep teams aligned and still get things shipped.

After completing my MBA, I moved to Vancouver and transitioned into the public sector, working as a Content Design Coordinator at HealthLink BC within the BC Government. The work shifted from campaigns to clarity — writing for millions of users, making health information accessible regardless of literacy level.

Now I'm deepening my project management expertise with a PMP certification, and looking for my next role in tech — somewhere I can bring together delivery rigour, design thinking, and genuine curiosity for data and impact.

Project Management UX Content Design Agile / Scrum Stakeholder Management Information Architecture WCAG Accessibility Figma Drupal CMS Google Analytics Digital Marketing PMP (In Progress) Plain Language Writing
